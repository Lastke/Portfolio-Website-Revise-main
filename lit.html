Background

This project I completed in my CareerFoundry UI Bootcamp project

    Project Overview
  • LIT is a mobile travel app that utilizes AI and ML algorithms to create personalized itineraries based on user preferences and location.

    • Problem Statement
  • Many travelers struggle with planning their itinerary, as it can be time-consuming and overwhelming to research and organize all the details. There is a need for a travel app that can create a personalized itinerary for users based on their preferences and interests, making the planning process easier and more convenient

    • Solution
  • The proposed idea is to develop a mobile travel app that incorporates artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms to create customized travel itineraries for users. The app will feature a user-friendly interface that enables users to input their travel dates, destination, budget, and interests, as well as their preferred mode of transportation and accommodation type.

Style Guide

The style guide for the LIT app follows a modern and clean design aesthetic, with a focus on simplicity and usability. The color palette consists of soothing blues and greens, representing travel and nature. The typography is clean and legible, with a combination of sans-serif and serif fonts.

Research

Existing travel apps lack efficient AI-powered itinerary planning capabilities. However, by harnessing the potential of AI technology, a new app can gather information and create personalized travel itineraries, revolutionizing the trip planning process for users. This innovative solution ensures convenience, time efficiency, and a tailored travel experience.

Challenge

Designing the AI travel app itinerary, LIT, poses challenges in UX and interface design. Accurate AI recommendations based on user preferences, intuitive interface creation, and continuous refinement through user feedback are key focuses.

Wireframes

To start with

The app will prompt users with questions upon entering to gain insight into their travel preferences and destination. This allows for personalized recommendations based on the user's input, or they can choose to skip and discover new places. This helps ensure that the app provides relevant and useful information to the user.

Progress

The app offers a two-fold approach to travel planning. Users can explore and select their desired travel destination, then browse through the available attractions, bars, restaurants, and hotels in that city. Alternatively, users can simply add a city to their travel plans, and the AI will recommend activities, dining options, and accommodations for them to consider. The app streamlines the process of planning a trip by suggesting recommendations and automatically adding them to the user's itinerary, ultimately saving them time and effort.
